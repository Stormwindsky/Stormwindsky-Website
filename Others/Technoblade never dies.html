1999 - 2022

About Alex

Alexander, known online as Technoblade, was an American YouTuber and Internet personality known for his Minecraft content. He was widely regarded as one of the best players in the community, famous for his skill, wit, and humble nature.

"If I had another hundred lives, I think I would choose to be Technoblade again every single time, as those were the happiest years of my life."

The video above, "so long nerds", was his final message to his fans, read by his father. It remains a testament to his legacy and the impact he had on millions.
